Diamond Wedding

The wedding of Lavish and Love was a truly unforgettable event. The couple, who are both successful entrepreneurs, spared no expense in creating a day that was both luxurious and romantic.



Some notes:

*Plan in detail: It is necessary to plan in detail the budget, time, location, and decoration style.
* Choose a reputable supplier: It is necessary to choose a reputable and experienced decoration service provider to ensure the quality of service.
*Supervise the construction: It is necessary to closely supervise the construction process to ensure progress and quality.

* Luxury Wedding Decoration:

Define the style:

* Romantic: Use pastel colors, fresh flowers, and candles.
*Elegant: Use gold, silver, black, and white tones, combined with crystals and glitter.
*Classic: Use deep colors, baroque patterns, and elaborate details.
*Modern: Use bright colors, minimalist lines, and incorporate modern technology.

*Choose the venue:

*Luxury banquet hall: Suitable for elegant and modern styles.
*Outdoor garden: Suitable for romantic and classic styles.
*Beach: Suitable for romantic and bohemian styles.

Decoration items:


* Flower gate: The flower gate is the first impression of the wedding.
*Gallery table: A place to display wedding photos, invitations, and guest gifts.



Diamond Wedding party food

Culinary style:

*European style: Luxurious and sophisticated with dishes such as cream of mushroom soup, pan-seared salmon, beef steak, etc.
* Asian style: Diverse and abundant with dishes such as spring rolls, dim sum, seafood hotpot, etc.

*Quality of ingredients:

* Use fresh, high-quality ingredients to ensure delicious flavor and safety for health.
* Prioritize local, seasonal ingredients to save costs and contribute to environmental protection.

*Dessert:

* Plated dessert: This is a traditional option where guests are served a plated dessert. Some popular options include cake, pie, and ice cream.
*Dessert bar: This is a more casual option where guests can choose their own dessert from a dessert bar. Some popular dessert bar options include cake, cookies, and brownies.

*Drinks :

*Open bar: This is an option where guests can choose from a variety of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.
*Cash bar: This is an option where guests pay for their drinks.
*Limited bar: This is an option where guests can choose from a limited selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.

* Notes :

Your budget: The cost of food can vary depending on the type of food you choose and the number of guests you are serving.
Here are some additional tips for planning your outdoor wedding menu:
Choose a caterer who has experience with outdoor weddings. Make sure the food is safe to eat in the heat or cold. Provide plenty of food and drinks for your guests. Have a backup plan in case of bad weather. With careful planning, you can create a delicious and memorable menu for your outdoor wedding.
